# Pricing Framework — Naik Kelas by Koneksi

> Panduan menetapkan harga kelas (self-paced, cohort, corporate) berbasis biaya, nilai, dan eksperimen.

## Framework 7 Langkah

1. **Definisikan outcome**: kelulusan + portofolio riil + peluang kerja/interview; segmen self-paced vs cohort; individual vs corporate.
2. **Hitung COGS per learner**: mentor per sesi × jumlah sesi ÷ rasio peserta/mentor; tool (video, storage), sertifikat, support.
3. **Tentukan target margin kontribusi**: B2C 60–70%, cohort 50–65%.
4. **Rumus floor price**: `price_floor = COGS / (1 − target_gross_margin)`.
5. **Benchmark kompetitor**: self-paced Rp150k–Rp1,5jt; cohort 6–12 minggu Rp3–15jt; corporate seat/tahun Rp1–5jt.
6. **Value-based pricing**: survei Van Westendorp dan Gabor-Granger; bundling (kelas + sertifikat + career service).
7. **Eksperimen & guardrails**: A/B harga 2–3 varian; monitor CR checkout, refund, completion, NPS, margin; revisi 6–8 minggu.

## Tier & Versi Produk

- **Self-paced**: konten + kuis.
- **Cohort Standard**: live session mingguan, review tugas ringan, komunitas.
- **Cohort Pro**: +1:1 mentor (jam terbatas), review mendalam, career coach.
- **Corporate**: seat/tahun, laporan HR, SLA, integrasi SSO/HRIS.
- **Add-on**: sertifikat terverifikasi, fast-track career, retake.

## Contoh Perhitungan (Cohort 8 Minggu)

- Asumsi:
  - Mentor: Rp300k/jam × 1,5 jam/sesi × 8 sesi = Rp3,6jt/mentor.
  - Rasio 1:25 → COGS mentor per peserta ≈ Rp144k.
  - Review & support: Rp250k/peserta.
  - Tool & sertifikat: Rp100k/peserta.
  - Total COGS per peserta ≈ Rp494k (bulatkan Rp500k).
- Target margin 65% → `price_floor ≈ 500k / (1 − 0,65) = Rp1,43jt`.
- Tier harga (uji A/B):
  - Cohort Standard: Rp1,5–2,0jt.
  - Cohort Pro: Rp3,0–3,5jt.
  - Self-paced: Rp250–500k.
  - Corporate seat (annual): Rp1,5–3jt/seat, min. 20 seat.

## Operasional 2 Minggu

- Kumpulkan COGS aktual (mentor, tool, sertifikat) per jalur.
- Jalankan survei WTP (≥100 responden target segmen).
- Siapkan 3 tier × 2 varian harga untuk A/B.
- Implementasi voucher/referral + integrasi cicilan.
- Tetapkan KPI: CR checkout, NPS, completion, refund, margin kontribusi.
